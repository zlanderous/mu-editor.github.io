Discuss Mu

It's good to talk, share knowledge and learn from other users of Mu. Perhaps you have a question, problem or are unsure how to do something with Mu? Discussing such things in a supportive environment is a good way to make progress, make friends and make a success of things. Put simply, Mu is a community focused project and we have created an online discussion group where the community can come together.

Community Conduct

We expect contributors to follow the Python Software foundation's Code of Conduct.

We won't hesitate to issue a clear warning and, if ignored, ban people who don't behave in the spirit of the code of conduct. We want our community to be a safe, welcoming and friendly place. If a conduct related situation arises, we will endeavour to act in a discreet, sympathetic and fair manner to all concerned.
